# Letter — Danvers, Oct. 7th 1863

Danvers, Oct. 7th 1863.

Dear Charles

I received your letter a few days ago. how can I express by words my astonishment at having such a letter. may I ask how long you were writing it? I judged that you were thinking of some thing else while you wrote... When I read your letter I could have given you a a scolding but have got over that now. what time do you find to read the daily News, and play. why don't you write oftener. how much do you study and where is your Greek. How do you stand in your studies. How many times can you speak the same piece. or how many times have you spoken "Stand up for the Flag". Who is Prof. Barrows.
